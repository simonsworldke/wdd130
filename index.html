Eldoret, Kenya

Eldoret is a vibrant town in western Kenya, known for its agricultural contributions and its cool, highland climate. It's one of the fastest-growing towns in Kenya, offering a mix of urban and rural life. Eldoret is also famous for its athletic heritage, as it has produced numerous world-class long-distance runners.
